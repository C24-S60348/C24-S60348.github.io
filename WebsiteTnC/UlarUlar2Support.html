Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I start a game?
A: From the main menu, select "Create Room" to start a new game. Choose your topic, enter your name, and share the room code with your friends. They can join by selecting "Join Room" and entering the code.
Q: How many players can play?
A: Ular Ular supports 2-4 players in each game room.
Q: Do I need to create an account?
A: No! Simply enter a nickname when you start playing. No registration, email, or password required.
Q: How do questions work?
A: When you land on certain spaces, you'll be asked a quiz question. Answer correctly to continue, or answer incorrectly and face the consequences! Questions are educational and fun.
Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: Yes, Ular Ular is an online multiplayer game and requires a stable internet connection to play.
Q: Is the game suitable for children?
A: Yes! Ular Ular is designed as an educational game suitable for all ages. The game contains no chat features, and we don't collect personal information beyond a temporary nickname.
Q: What happens to my data?
A: Your nickname and game data exist only during your active game session. All data is automatically deleted when the game ends. We don't store any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Can I play offline?
A: No, Ular Ular requires an internet connection as it's an online multiplayer game that connects players in real-time.
Q: The game is not loading or connecting. What should I do?
A: Make sure you have a stable internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, please contact us using the information below.
Q: Can I suggest new features or topics?
A: Absolutely! We'd love to hear your suggestions. Please contact us via email with your ideas.
