Information Collection and Use

Funderive is primarily an offline educational game focused on improving math skills through quizzes and challenges. Most data — such as progress, scores, and local settings — are stored only on your device and never uploaded or shared.

For certain quizzes, the app may send question and answer data online to check results or retrieve new quiz content. This data is transmitted anonymously — no personal information such as names, emails, device IDs, or account details are collected or associated with it.

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The limited online communication is used solely to exchange anonymous quiz data and improve the educational experience.
