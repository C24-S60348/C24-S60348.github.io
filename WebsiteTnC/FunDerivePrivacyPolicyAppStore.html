Privacy Policy

AF1 Productions built the Funderive app as an educational math game. This iOS version of the app is distributed via the Apple App Store and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information for anyone who chooses to use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

Funderive is primarily an offline educational game focused on improving math skills through quizzes and challenges. Most data — such as progress, scores, and local settings — are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared by us.

For certain quizzes, the app may send question and answer data online to check results or retrieve new quiz content. This data is transmitted anonymously — no personal information such as names, emails, device identifiers, or account details are collected or associated with it.

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The limited online communication is used solely to exchange anonymous quiz data and improve the educational experience.

Third-Party Services

Funderive may use trusted third-party services (for example, Apple services or analytics providers) to help us understand app performance or deliver core functionality. These services may collect limited technical information as part of their normal operation, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any relevant third-party services that may be used with this app.

Children’s Privacy

Funderive is designed for educational purposes and can be safely used by children. Since the app does not collect personal data, parents and guardians can be confident that their child’s personal information remains private.

Security

We value your trust in using our app. Although we do not collect personal information, any data transmitted (such as quiz content) is handled using reasonable security practices to help provide a safe user experience.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
