Funderive Support

Thank you for using Funderive, an educational math game created by AF1 Productions. If you are experiencing any issues with the app or have questions, we are here to help.

How to Contact Us

For support, feedback, or bug reports, please contact us at:

Email: af1gaming01@gmail.com

What to Include in Your Message

To help us resolve your issue more efficiently, please include (if possible):

  • The device model (for example, iPhone 17 Pro or iPad model)
  • The operating system version (for example, iOS version)
  • A brief description of the problem
  • Any steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots, if relevant

We aim to respond to support requests as soon as reasonably possible.

For information on how we handle data and privacy, please see our Privacy Policy.
