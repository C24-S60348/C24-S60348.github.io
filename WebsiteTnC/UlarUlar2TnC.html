Introduction

Welcome to Ular Ular ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our educational Snakes and Ladders multiplayer game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for:

  • Game Functionality: To enable multiplayer gameplay and display player information during game sessions
  • Educational Purposes: To track question responses and provide educational feedback
  • Game Management: To manage game rooms and coordinate turns between players

Data Storage and Retention

  • Temporary Storage: All game data is stored temporarily only for the duration of the game session
  • Automatic Deletion: Game session data, including nicknames and game progress, is automatically deleted when:
    • The game ends
    • Players leave the game room
    • The session expires due to inactivity
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not maintain databases of user information or game history beyond active sessions

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Other Players: Your nickname and game actions are visible to other players in the same game room
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes
  • No Analytics Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Children's Privacy

Ular Ular is an educational game suitable for children. We comply with children's privacy protection regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond a temporary nickname
  • No account creation or parental consent is required
  • No persistent tracking or profiling of users
  • The game contains no chat features or direct communication between players

Parents and guardians should supervise their children's use of the App and ensure they understand not to share personal information through their nickname.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the temporary data processed during game sessions:

  • Secure server communications
  • Data encryption in transit
  • No permanent storage of personal information
  • Automatic session expiration

However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Nickname Choice: Choose any nickname you prefer (we recommend not using your real name)
  • Game Participation: You can leave any game session at any time
  • No Tracking: We do not track your activity across game sessions

Online Multiplayer

Ular Ular is an online multiplayer game that:

  • Shares your nickname and game actions with other players in your room
  • Does not provide direct communication features (no chat or messaging)
